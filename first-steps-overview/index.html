The Overview

The overview sheet shows the balance of all people, the currencies with their respective rate and the list of responses with calculations.

People, added in the Person row will be synced into the form.

Currencies, added in the Currency row will be synced into the form. The corresponding balance is used to convert between different currencies.

The Responses show replies from the form and the expenses for each person.
